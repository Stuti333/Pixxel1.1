ABOUT

This is the world of Imaginations and Visual Thinking. Graphics can help with the expression of ideas, which can bind a program together for presentation at various avenues. Be it a logo design, a self-portrait illustration, or building your 2D and 3D scenes, Graficas is a helpful companion in your journeys. Apart from core visual designing related works, the hub also focuses on designing UI, UX, visual effects, and others.


This hub is highly committed with designing excellence, offering a unique designing experience to the students to enhance their imaginative and creative aspects. The aim is to inculcate the visual aspects as designs, logos, photographs, illustrations, animation, filters and effects, and others. The Hub also introduces the usage of key design principles and visual elements to present a visual composition.
